This app is a wine cellar manager. Features:<ul>
<li>Listing of your bottles, with characteristics (appellation, domain, cuvee, vintage and a few others)</li>
<li>Easy stock management</li>
<li>Possibility to add a photo</li>
<li>Full database export/import (with photos)</li>
<li>CSV export to handle the listing in another software (so as to print a paper listing, for example)</li>
<li>Type filter</li>
<li>Many pre-defined sort options.</li></ul>

What's new in v1.1 ?
Database is now saved when leaving the bottle editor, so that it is saved even though you quit the app by killing it
